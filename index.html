What I'd work on first
01Flexible Record Mental ModelCore UX
Attio's power is that any object can be modelled any way. The UX problem is helping new users understand that without requiring them to understand relational data first. Research the moment users first grasp the model and then design backwards from it.
02AI Enrichment Trust LayerAI UX
As Attio auto-fills relationship context from emails, calls, and product data, users need a reliable way to know what the CRM surfaced vs. what they entered. The ownership question changes how confidently people act on the data.
03Workflow Builder LegibilityInteraction
Attio's automation engine is powerful but building a workflow is still a developer-adjacent task. The UX problem is making trigger-action logic legible to a non-technical GTM operator without losing expressiveness.
